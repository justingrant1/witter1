Our Story

Meet Seth Chandler

Chief Numismatist & Owner

Witter Coin was founded in 1960 by legendary numismatist Dean Witter, who opened a small store in San Francisco's Financial District. Today, Seth Chandler leads one of the most dynamic coin shops in America — with 28 employees and a mission to serve collectors at every level.

Seth has been recognized multiple times by Coin World as one of the Top 100 Numismatists of All Time and named one of the "Greatest Coin Graders of All Time" by COINage magazine. He is a contributor to the iconic Red Book and serves on the Board of Governors for the ANA.
