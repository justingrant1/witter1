FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment? expand_more
No appointment is needed for most visits. Walk-ins are always welcome during store hours. For larger collections (over 100 coins, or estate collections), we recommend calling ahead so we can allocate enough time to properly evaluate everything.
Is the appraisal really free? expand_more
Absolutely. Witter Coin has been offering free appraisals since 1960 for anyone who visits our showroom. There is no charge and no obligation to sell. "I just want to know what it's worth" is always a welcome reason to visit.
How much do you pay? expand_more
We buy collections ranging from a few dollars to well over $1,000,000. Our offers are based on current market values and our 65 years of expertise. We are known for paying fair, competitive prices — it's how we've stayed in business since 1960.
What if I don't want to sell? expand_more
That's completely fine. We will appraise your items, answer your questions, and you are free to take them home. There is never any pressure to sell. Many people visit just to learn what their coins are worth — we welcome that.
Do you buy items other than coins? expand_more
Yes! We buy gold and silver bullion, old gold jewelry, Rolex watches, paper money, and other collectibles. If you're unsure whether we'd be interested, just call or bring it in — we're happy to take a look.
